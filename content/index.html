Great sights with great people

We are arranging JourneyZone on September 13th-15th, the first weekend after JavaZone. This is our fourth annual journey into the Norwegian wilderness. We would like to exclusively invite speakers to explore the Norwegian nature, enjoy the very best of Norwegian food and have a great time together.

To ensure safety and exclusivity, this journey is limited to 30 participants on a first come, first served basis. JavaZone will subsidize the journey with qualified guides to ensure the best possible experience. Your personal expenses will be around NOK 1500. More information about the trips destination, departure and arrival times to Oslo will be provided.

Soak up some inspiration

To see what this is all about, take a look at the pic­tures from last years JourneyZone. JourneyZone has been a tradition for many years, here are some pictures from Jour­ney­Zone 2010. Some ad­di­tional pic­tures can be found in Com­put­er­world (nor­we­gian).

How to register

JourneyZone is a trip exclusively for speakers of full presentations. We will send out information to the speakers on how to register for the trip after the program is finished.
