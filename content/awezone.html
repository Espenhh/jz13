AweZone Festival 2013

After a long day learning great new stuff at the Conference, we all need to chill out and have a nice, cold beer. We meet at the Rockefeller Music Hall to listen to some great live music.

In the main concert hall, we'll be joined by some of our partners' great bands. Who they are has yet to decided, but judging from previous years, they will bring their best jams.

  • When and where? 11th September 2013, Rockefeller Music Hall, Torggata 16, Oslo.
  • Who is invited? Everyone with a normal JavaZone ticket. Special tickets for AweZone will be sold.
  • How much does it cost? Free as in lunch! Entrance to the party is included in your regular ticket.
