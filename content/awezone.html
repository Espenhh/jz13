Join us at this years AweZone after-party.
Ain't no party like a great geek party!

Freezing beers, great bands, awesome people

After a long day learning great new stuff at the Conference, we all need to chill out and have a nice, cold beer. We meet up at Rockefeller Music Hall to party, mingle and listen to some great bands.

In the main concert hall, we'll be joined by some of our partners' great bands. Who they are are not yet desided, but judging from previous years, they will bring their best jam and rock the building.

In addition to this, we'll book a few bands that do that music-thing for a living. Previous years, we have been delighed to have great bands such as CC Cowboys, Honningbarna, Oslo Ess & Turboneger. This year will be no different!

Want to rock Rockefeller?

A great party is nothing without great bands! And any respectful IT-conference needs great geek-bands. That's why we want you up on the main stage!

If your company has a great band, this is your opportunity to show yourself to the world. Just send us a small pitch, including anything that makes you look good (pictures, sound-clips, videos), and we'll get in touch with you.

Send us your pitch today!

  • Time and place 11th September 2013, Rockefeller Music Hall, Torggata 16, Oslo.
  • Who is invited Everyone with a normal JavaZone ticket. Special tickets for AweZone will also be sold.
  • Cost Free as in lunch! We'll even sponsor the first rounds of beers.
