Join us at this years AweZone after-party!
Ain't no party like a great geek party!

Freezing beers, great bands, awesome people

After a long day learning great new stuff at the Conference, we all need to chill out and have a nice, cold beer. We meet at the Rockefeller Music Hall to party, mingle and listen to some great live music. In the main concert hall, we'll be joined by some of our partners' great bands. Who they are has yet to decided, but judging from previous years, they will bring their best jams and rock the building.

In addition to this, we'll book in a few bands that do that music-thing for a living. Over the past few years we have been delighed to present great bands such as CC Cowboys, Honningbarna, Oslo Ess & Turboneger. This year will be no different!

Want to rock Rockefeller?

A great party is nothing without great bands! And any respectful IT-conference needs great geek-bands. That's why we want you up on the main stage! If your company has a great band, this is your opportunity to show yourself to the world. Just send a small pitch to javazone@​java.​no, including anything that makes you look good (pictures, sound-clips, videos), and we'll get in touch with you.

Photos by Avdeling 4-fotografene.

  • When and where? 11th September 2013, Rockefeller Music Hall, Torggata 16, Oslo.
  • Who is invited? Everyone with a normal JavaZone ticket. Special tickets for AweZone will be sold.
  • How much does it cost? Free as in lunch! Entrance to the party is included in your regular ticket.
