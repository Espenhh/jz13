Top talks JavaZone 2012

After the JavaZone conference we ask the participants to rate the presentations they attend. These are the top talks at JavaZone 2012 according to that rating.

Top 5 presentations

  1. Advanced Git (Tim Berglund)
  2. Everything a Java(script) programmer needs to know about internet video (Eirik Bjørsnøs)
  3. Verden er urettferdig (Aleksander Blomskøld & Rune Peter Bjørnstad)
  4. Networks for Programmers (Ken Sipe)
  5. Zombie TDD: Live parprogrammering (Christian Johansen & Magnar Sveen)

Top 3 lightning talks

  1. Avinstaller Java nå! (Jostein Tveit)
  2. Readable code - and the long lost secret of how to achieve it (Christin Gorman)
  3. Sikker lagring av passord (Jon Are Rakvåg)
